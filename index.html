How it works

From inbox to quote, without re-typing a single lane.

1

Paste the RFQ

Drop the raw RFQ email in. Echelon extracts every lane — origin, destination, mode — and discards the greetings and headers.

2

Match carrier rates

Each lane is matched against your pre-negotiated carrier rate sheets by origin, destination, and rail/port designation.

3

Track and quote

Enter rates as carriers confirm them. Echelon tracks what's quoted, what's pending, and builds your rate history lane by lane.
